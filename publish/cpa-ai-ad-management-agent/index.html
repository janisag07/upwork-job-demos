1. Creative Generation

Generate tax-prep, tax-planning, and backlog-service ad copy with compliance guardrails: no misleading IRS claims, no guaranteed outcomes, CPA review required.

2. API Monitoring

Pull daily spend, CPL, conversion rate, lead quality, and campaign/source performance from Google Ads, Meta Ads, and GoHighLevel attribution data.

3. Human Approval Layer

Recommendations are queued for approve/reject first. The AI can suggest actions, but cannot spend money or change campaigns without authorization.
