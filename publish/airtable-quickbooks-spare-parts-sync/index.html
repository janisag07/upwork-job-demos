Airtable ↔ QuickBooks Spare Parts Sync

A spare-parts inventory sync blueprint for keeping Airtable and QuickBooks aligned without manual stock/cost updates.

Workflow modules

Inventory Source MapAirtable parts table defines SKU/part number, stock count, cost, supplier, reorder status and repair-order usage.
QuickBooks Sync LogicNew items, stock adjustments and costs are pushed to QuickBooks with stable IDs and reconciliation notes.
Repair/Sale EventsWhen a part is used in a repair order or sold, Airtable records the event and the automation updates QBO stock levels.
Exception QueueMissing SKU, duplicate part, API error or cost mismatch goes to a review table instead of silently corrupting inventory.
Bilingual HandoffSpanish-facing labels and operating notes can be prepared for the client communication flow.
