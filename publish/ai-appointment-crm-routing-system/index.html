AI Appointment + CRM Routing System

A modular AI appointment system that routes customer messages into calendar actions, CRM updates, lead triage, and safe human handoff.

appointment bookingreschedule/cancel/confirmCRM/database updateshuman escalationmultilingual routinglogs/error handling

Workflow modules

Message IntakeWebsite form/chat or messaging channel captures request, language, contact details, requested service, urgency and preferred time.
Intent RouterAI classifies appointment, reschedule, cancellation, confirmation, reminder, lead, support, or unclear intent before any action is taken.
Calendar + CRM SyncApproved appointment actions update calendar slots and CRM/database records with status, source, notes and next-step tasks.
Safety + EscalationUnclear, risky, duplicate or exception cases are logged and routed to a human queue instead of guessing.
Reusable Business TemplateConfiguration layer for clinics, dental practices, beauty centers, real estate, restaurants, workshops, gyms and service businesses.
