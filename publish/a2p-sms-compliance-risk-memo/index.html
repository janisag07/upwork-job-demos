Proof-of-approach for the Upwork brief

A2P SMS Notification Risk Assessment Memo

Consulting-style decision board for multi-location transactional SMS notifications: 500+ client websites, 3k–10k daily texts, Twilio/GHL architecture, A2P 10DLC questions, and deliverability risk controls.

Use case

Transactional/internal alerts

Lead form submission → designated owner/manager/sales rep gets notification; not positioned as marketing blasts.

Scale

3k–10k texts/day

Volume plan needs throttling, segmentation, suppression, and monitoring rather than one fragile number.

Honesty

Architecture + researched review

I would clarify edge cases first and avoid pretending carrier approval is guaranteed.

Architecture question

Decide whether client sites should share one master sending identity, use brand/campaign grouping, or segment numbers by business/location depending on ownership and message content.

Compliance checklist

Brand/campaign registration, message sample classification, opt-out language, internal recipient consent, lead-form disclosure, throttling, and audit trail.

Provider decision

Compare Twilio, Telnyx, toll-free, long-code, and GHL constraints against registration effort, scale, deliverability, support, and routing flexibility.

Deliverable

60–90 minute consult plus written recommendation document: suggested architecture, risks, provider choice, and rollout steps.

Consulting preview only; final A2P/10DLC recommendation depends on exact brands, opt-in flow, message samples, provider accounts, and current carrier rules.
