What this first version would include

Natural Voice Intake

Greets callers, understands requested service, asks the right qualification questions, and avoids robotic scripts.

FAQ + Policy Answers

Handles pricing ranges, service descriptions, location, hours, prep notes, and common med-spa questions.

Calendar Booking

Checks availability and books directly into Google Calendar or Calendly with confirmation details.

Human Transfer / Message

Transfers urgent or complex calls to staff, or captures a clean message when no one is available.

Call Summary Log

Stores caller, service interest, intent, booked slot, transcript summary, and follow-up status in Sheets/CRM.

Testing + Guardrails

Includes realistic call tests, escalation rules, banned medical claims, and confidence thresholds.
