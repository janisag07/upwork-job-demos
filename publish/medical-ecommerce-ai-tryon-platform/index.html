AI Medical E-Commerce Platform Architecture

A premium app architecture for a Canadian medical wig ecommerce platform: catalog, AI virtual try-on, consultation booking, checkout, customer accounts, and admin operations as one staged build instead of a vague monolith.

Catalog→Try-on consent→AI recommendation→Booking→Checkout→Admin dashboard
