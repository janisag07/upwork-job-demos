Proof-of-approach for a Meta / WhatsApp Business registration fix

WhatsApp Registration Debug Board

A compact diagnostic path for the exact issue: the phone number is added in Facebook Developer / Meta, but WhatsApp Business registration fails. The goal is to find whether the break is business verification, WABA ownership, phone-number status, app permissions, or webhook/test-message setup.

Registration failure checklist

1
Business + app stateConfirm Meta Business verification, app mode, products enabled, correct Business Manager, and no hidden policy/account restriction.
2
WABA and phone ownershipCheck whether the WhatsApp Business Account is correctly linked, whether the number is owned/available, and whether it is already attached elsewhere.
3
Registration error captureRecord the exact failed registration step, error text/code, screenshot state, and the Meta object IDs needed for support/escalation.
4
Permissions + token pathVerify app permissions, system user/access token scope, phone_number_id, waba_id, callback URL, webhook subscription, and test endpoint access.
5
Fix or escalation notesApply the obvious fix when possible; otherwise deliver the exact escalation path with IDs, screenshots, and action list for Meta support.

What I would not overpromise

No fake guarantee

Client-provided accounts

Clear evidence trail
Every finding is documented so the client knows what was checked and what remains blocked.
Safe testing
Test messages/webhooks only after registration and permissions are confirmed.
