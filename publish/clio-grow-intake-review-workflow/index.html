Pipeline stages

Simple Clio Grow stages built for fast triage and clean reporting.

1

New lead

Auto-created from form/call/Zap. Conflict check flag, source tag, practice area, urgency.

2

Consult scheduled

Calendar confirmation, fee/consult instructions, reminders, no-show rescue.

3

Consult complete

Attorney notes, fit assessment, fee quote, retainer link, follow-up deadline.

4

Retained

Engagement packet complete, matter created in Clio Manage, review trigger deferred until close.

5

Declined

Reason captured: no fit, budget, conflict, no response, DIY, hired another firm.

Automated follow-up

Email/SMS sequences that use the attorney's copy.

New lead first response

  1. Immediate email: received + what happens next.
  2. Immediate SMS: short confirmation + booking link.
  3. Task: staff reviews conflict/urgency within business hours.

Consult scheduled

  1. Email confirmation with Zoom/office details.
  2. 24-hour SMS reminder.
  3. 2-hour SMS reminder with reschedule link.

Consult complete → retainer

  1. Same-day email with next steps and engagement packet.
  2. Day 2 SMS: answer questions / reminder.
  3. Day 5 task: attorney follow-up if high-value fit.

No response rescue

  1. Day 1 polite check-in.
  2. Day 3 final scheduling prompt.
  3. Day 7 mark stale unless replied; keep source reporting intact.
Trigger timing

Rules that avoid awkward or premature messages.

Intake automations fire immediately only after source + consent fields are present; staff task remains the safeguard for family-law urgency and conflict review.

Consent-aware SMS

SMS steps are conditional on opt-in/consent fields and can be disabled per matter.

Zapier fallback

If native forms cannot map a source cleanly, Zapier normalizes source, campaign, and referral fields.

Post-matter review intercept

Private feedback first, then route based on sentiment.

1. Matter closed in Clio Manage

Trigger waits a configurable buffer after close (for example 2–7 days) and checks exclusions such as sensitive outcomes or attorney hold.

2. Private feedback survey

Short form: rating, comments, permission question. Responses stay private unless the client chooses public review.

3A. Positive route → Google

4–5 star / positive response shows one-click Google Business Profile review link and thank-you email.

3B. Critical route → private

1–3 star / concern response routes to a private attorney notification and internal issue form, not a public ask.

4. Follow-up task

Critical feedback creates a Clio task for attorney review; positive feedback is logged for source/outcome insight.

5. Documentation

Maintained with editable templates, trigger map, field glossary, and QA checklist.
