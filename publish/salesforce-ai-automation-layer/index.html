Salesforce AI Automation Layer for Recruiting Operations

A compact architecture preview for connecting Salesforce REST API, n8n, Supabase logs, Claude structured evaluation, and a dashboard into one controlled automation layer — not three disconnected scripts.

Workflow 1
Bounced Email Cleanup

Queue bounced contacts, research replacements, validate email confidence, detect job changes, then update Salesforce with audit notes.

Workflow 2
AI Lead Generation

Pull company/career-page signals, evaluate fit with structured LLM outputs, dedupe Accounts/Contacts/Leads before Salesforce writes.

Workflow 3
7,000+ Page Matching

Batch crawl customer career pages, match candidate CV signals to open roles, store logs and human-review queues in Supabase.
