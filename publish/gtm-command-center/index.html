Personal Portfolio Intelligence Briefing for a GTM Advisor

A daily command center that pulls signals from Slack, Gmail, Gong and Otter.ai across Keboola, HiveMQ and Duvo.ai, then sends one clean morning briefing with risks, asks, follow-ups and board-level priorities.

Slack signalsChannel mentions, blockers, launch updates, customer escalations.
Gmail signalsInvestor/client asks, unread priority threads, follow-up deadlines.
Gong + OtterCall summaries, objections, next steps, buyer/account mentions.
Inbox briefingOne organized email by company, urgency and action owner.
First milestone: connect one company + two sources, generate the daily briefing format, then expand source-by-source safely.

Implementation Plan

1
Source map + access model
Define companies, channels, labels, Gong/Otter folders, privacy boundaries and what should never enter the briefing.
2
Ingestion + normalization
Pull daily deltas from Slack/Gmail/Gong/Otter into a normalized signal table with source links and timestamps.
3
AI ranking + summarization
Classify each signal as risk, opportunity, follow-up, decision or context; dedupe repeated mentions across tools.
4
Morning email + QA log
Send one brief every morning with confidence labels, original-source references and a simple error/debug log.
