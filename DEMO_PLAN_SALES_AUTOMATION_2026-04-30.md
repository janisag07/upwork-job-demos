# Demo Plan — AI Sales Pipeline Automation

Purpose: reusable Upwork proof asset for CRM / sales ops / AI automation jobs.

## Demo name
AI Sales Pipeline Automation: Gmail → AI Triage → CRM → Draft Reply → Follow-up

## Target jobs
Use for fixed-price Upwork jobs involving:
- Gmail/Outlook inbox automation
- CRM setup or cleanup: Pipedrive, HubSpot, Close, GoHighLevel, Airtable
- Lead qualification / routing / response drafting
- Sales follow-up automation
- n8n / Make / Zapier / OpenAI workflows

## Buyer pain
Clients lose revenue because inbound leads are handled slowly, manually, or inconsistently. The demo proves Janis can build a system that captures leads, classifies urgency, creates/updates CRM records, drafts replies, and schedules follow-ups.

## Demo requirements
- Looks like a real sales ops command center.
- Interactive controls must change outputs:
  - Lead source selector
  - AI confidence/routing result
  - CRM card
  - Draft reply
  - Follow-up plan
  - ROI/response-time metrics
- Visible fictional-data disclaimer.
- No screenshots needed in proposals; direct URL goes into cover letter.
